Our entry for the 2024 Boost Digital sustainability hackathon. The aim was to come up with a more efficient way to read QR codes on mobile. Our plan was to port over & compile the quirc C-library for Android using Android's Native Development Kit (NDK), and then embed this in a QR code reading application developed using React Native. This project was never finished, due to a 48h hackathon time limit.
